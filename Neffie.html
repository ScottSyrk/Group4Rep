Cornbread Stuffing


It's that time of year again, and nothing goes better with the main course than stuffing.

Here is a recipe that is sure to be a big hit.


Ingredients

  • 1 box jiffy cornbread mix
  • 2 standard boxes of dry stuffing mix
  • 1 16oz jimmy dean sausage roll

Instructions



Cook cornbread according to instructions given on the box. Once done let cool for 15 minutes. In a frying pan use half of the sausage roll cook until golden brown. When done set aside to cool. Now take your prepared cornbread and crumble it up into a cake pan. Add the sausage and dry stuffing mix. Add 1 cup of hot water mix it all up. turn oven on to 350 and place stuffing on middle rack for 30 minutes.
